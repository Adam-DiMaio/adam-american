Harbinger 3U CubeSat

Earth Water Observation with Innovative Antenna Module & Earth Station

Project Overview

This Harbinger 3U CubeSat Communications Capstone Project presents the Design and Implementation of an Autonomous Earth-Based Uplink/Downlink Station with a Complementary RF Orbital Module to support Reliable Satellite Communication. The Combination of these Systems Aspire to Advance the Continuous Environmental Monitoring of Water Distribution that may Not be Visible from the Ground.

Goals

  • Autonomously Track and Establish Communication with Orbital Satellites.
  • Integrate seamlessly with Utah Tech’s Infrastructure to allow for Remote Access.
  • Ensure the Final Product is Easy to Use, Intuitive for Demonstrations, and an Effective Educational Tool.
  • Manufacture all Componenets In-House for RF Orbital Module to Ensure Tight Tolerances and High Reliability.

    • Key Contributions & Responsibilities

    • Contributed to both Antenna Module & Earth Station Design with Integration.
    • Ensured Compliance with FCC Regulations and Manufacturing Guidelines.
    • Collaborated with a Multidisciplinary Team to Mechanically Optimize Satellite Functionality.

    Skills

    • Mechanical Engineering – Small Satellite Mechanical Design
    • Manufacturing – Antenna & Earth Station Fabrication
    • Multidisciplinary Collaboration & Integration – Combining Mechanical Components with Electrical Systems

    Outcome

    The CubeSat project is ongoing and has significantly advanced my understanding of Small Satellite Commuication Systems and their Optimal Designs.
