Mini Electronic Paddle Boats

Hydrodynamic Efficiency & Electronic Control

Project Overview

This project involved designing and testing small-scale electronic paddle boats focused on achieving hydrodynamic efficiency and precise control.

Key Contributions & Responsibilities

  • Designed boat models using CAD and simulated performance.
  • Analyzed fluid dynamics to optimize paddle efficiency.
  • Integrated electronic motor control systems for maneuverability.

Skills & Technologies

  • CAD & Simulation: Boat modeling & performance analysis
  • Fluid Dynamics: Evaluating hydrodynamic forces
  • Electronics: Motor control systems integration

Outcome

The prototypes demonstrated enhanced efficiency and control, paving the way for further innovations in small-scale electronic watercraft design.
