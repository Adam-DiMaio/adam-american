Heat Transfer: Heat Sink

Heat Transfer & Dissipation Thermal Analysis/p>

Project Overview

This Project Focused on Analyzing and Design Creation for Heat Dissipation in a Complex Structure Heat Sink Through Simulation, Fabrication, and Real-World Testing.

Key Contributions & Responsibilities

  • Conducted ANSYS Discovery Thermal Simulations to Evaluate Heat Distribution from the CAD Designed Heat Sink.
  • Assessed Material Properties & Circular Fin Design/Layout to Improve Conduction & Dissipation.
  • Fabricated the Designed Heat Sink Using Aluminum Sand Casting and Polycast 3D Filament.

Skills & Technologies

  • Thermal Analysis: Simulation of Heat Transfer & Dissipation
  • Heat Transfer: Evaluating Conductive Properties & Calculating Fin Heat Transfer Rate
  • Fabrication: Sand Casting & 3D Prototyping

Outcome

This Project Enhanced our Understanding of Herat Transfer Rate Across Fins and see how Using Simulations Helps us get Better Results. We Delivered a Heat Sink Design with Improved Heat Dissipation Efficiency, Enhancing the Performance of our Heat Sink.
