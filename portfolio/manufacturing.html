Manufacturing: Coffee Grinder

Injection Molding, CNC Fabrication, & Product Design

Project Overview

This Manufacturing Project Involved Designing and Manufacturing a Custom Coffee Grinder, which Focused on Creating a Manufacturable, Durable, and Functional Design through Injection Molding, CNC Machining, Manual Milling, and Manual Lathing. The Highlight of the Coffee Grinder’s Design is the Injection-Molded Adjustment Knob, which Allows Users to Fine-Tune their Coffee Grain Size.

Key Contributions & Responsibilities

  • Designed the Coffee Grinder’s Internal Components, Ensuring Proper Tolerances to fit the Clear PVC Tube for a Smooth Grinding Mechanism.
  • Created a Custom Injection Mold for the Adjustment Knob, using CNC-Milled Aluminum to Achieve Precise Dimensions.
  • Selected Food Grade Fasteners and Materials for the Coffee Grinder to Allow for Coffee Beans.
  • Used Manual Mill and Lathe to Achieve Tight Tolerances on the End Caps, Threaded Shaft and the Middle Piece Holding the Spring for the Adjustment Knob.
  • Verified the Final Assembly’s Functionality through Tterative Testing and End-User Feedback, Focusing on Consistent Grind Size and Ease of Use.

Skills & Technologies

  • Injection Molding: Design of Mold Cavities, Gating Systems, and Idealized Material Flow for Plastic Part Production.
  • CNC Machining: Milled Aluminum Mold Insert to Precise Tolerances, Ensuring the Final Injection-Molded Parts fit Correctly.
  • Manual Machining: Used both the Manual Mill and Lathe to Achieve Tight Tolerances for Aluminum Threaded Shaft, End Caps, and Middle Piece for Adjustment Knob Spring.
  • Product Design: Developed Ergonomic Features and Mechanical Assemblies that Withstand Repeated use in Coffee Grinding.
  • Testing & Validation: Conducted Trials to Ensure Consistent Grind Quality, Durable Components, and User-Friendly Operation.

Outcome

This Completed Coffee Grinder Featured a Robust, Injection-Molded Adjustment Knob and Precision-Machined Components, which Resulted in a Reliable, User-Friendly Product. Through Careful Material Selection, Mold Design, and Iterative Testing, the Final Grinder Offered a Consistent Grind Quality while Showcasing Advanced Manufacturing Techniques and Attention to Detail.
