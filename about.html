About Me

❮ ❯

Hi, I'm Adam DiMaio

I'm a passionate Mechanical Engineer specializing in Design, Manufacturing, CAD, and FEA. With a Bachelors of Science from Utah Tech University and hands-on experience across a variety of projects—from wearable medical devices to high-speed train infrastructure—I transform complex challenges into innovative solutions.

My journey in engineering has allowed me to work in multidisciplinary teams, honing my skills in CAD modeling, finite element analysis, and modern machining techniques. I’m committed to continuous learning and leveraging technology to solve real-world problems.

Whether it’s developing a CubeSat for Earth observation or contributing to groundbreaking transportation projects, I bring creativity, technical expertise, and a collaborative spirit to every challenge.

If I am not doing Engineering then I am either Weightlifting which I love to do being a former Division 1 Athlete or spending time with Family and Friends.
